Instructions

Welcome in amiibo simulator for Zelda Breath of the Wild!

  • You can choose which amiibo and the number of scans you want.
  • Select scan size (only for a few amiibos like Urbosa and Daruk).
  • To calculate probabilities, the site performs thousands of simulations. The greater the number of simulations you select, the more accurate the probabilities will be. The time it takes for the simulator to calculate probabilities depends on the number you choose and the computing power of your device. Recommended number of simulations: 200,000.
  • The site calculates the probability of having at least the selected items in the desired number of scans.

Use the Reset button to clear all selections.
